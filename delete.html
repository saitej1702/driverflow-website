Delete Your Account

Learn how to delete your DriveFlo account and data

Important: Account Deletion is Permanent

Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data including:

  • All mileage entries and trip logs
  • All expense records
  • All uploaded receipt images
  • Your profile and vehicle information
  • All generated reports and history

This action cannot be undone. Please export your data before deleting your account if you need to keep records for tax purposes.

Method 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

The easiest way to delete your account is directly from the DriveFlo app:

  1. Open DriveFlo

    Launch the DriveFlo app on your device

  2. Go to Settings

    Tap the gear icon in the top right corner or navigate to Settings from the menu

  3. Scroll to "Account" section

    Find the Account management options at the bottom of settings

  4. Tap "Delete Account"

    This option is shown in red text

  5. Confirm Deletion

    Read the warning and confirm by tapping "Delete My Account"

Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately upon confirmation.

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion by email, you can contact us directly:

Send an email to:

support@drivefloapp.com

Please include in your email:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • The email address associated with your DriveFlo account
  • Your full name (as registered in the app)
  • Confirmation that you understand this action is permanent

We will process your deletion request within 7 business days and send a confirmation email once complete.

What Data Gets Deleted?

Permanently Deleted

  • • Your account credentials
  • • Profile information
  • • Vehicle details
  • • All mileage entries
  • • All expense records
  • • All receipt images
  • • Subscription history (in our system)
  • • App preferences and settings

Retained Separately

  • • Google Play/App Store purchase records (managed by Google/Apple)
  • • Any data you exported before deletion
  • • Anonymized analytics data that cannot identify you

To cancel your subscription, visit your Google Play or App Store subscription settings.

Don't Forget: Cancel Your Subscription

Deleting your DriveFlo account does not automatically cancel your subscription. To avoid future charges, you must cancel your subscription separately:

Google Play (Android)

  1. Open Google Play Store
  2. Tap Menu → Subscriptions
  3. Find DriveFlo
  4. Tap Cancel subscription

App Store (iOS)

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name → Subscriptions
  3. Find DriveFlo
  4. Tap Cancel Subscription

Export Your Data First

Before deleting your account, we recommend exporting your data for your records:

  1. Open DriveFlo and go to the Export tab
  2. Select date range for the data you want to export
  3. Choose export format:
    • PDF - Formatted reports for tax filing
    • CSV - Spreadsheet format for your records
  4. Download your exports and save them to a secure location

Note: Export features require an active Pro subscription. If your subscription has expired, contact support for a data export.

Data Retention After Deletion

Upon account deletion:

  • Your personal data is deleted from our active systems immediately
  • Backup systems are purged within 30 days
  • Anonymized, non-identifiable analytics data may be retained for service improvement
  • We may retain certain information if required by law (e.g., transaction records for tax compliance)

For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.
